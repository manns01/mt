About Me

My name is Manish Kumar Tamta. Currently, I am doing M.S. (physics) at IISc Bangalore. I have done my undergrad viz. B.Sc. (H) Physics from St. Stephen's College, University of Delhi. My research interests include Astrophysics and Cosmology. Currently, I am doing a research project in computational astrophysics in which I study the physics of astrophysical systems with hydrodynamic simulations. I like fluid mechanics a lot. Apart from physics, I enjoy traveling and photography.
